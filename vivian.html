Vivian Nwokonko was born on June 22nd, 1990. She was born into the family of Ichie and Lolo Samuel Ezeobi.She hails from Umudiala, Ezinifite, in Nnewi South Local Government Area, Anambra State, Nigeria. She comes from a family of ten.

She started her primary school education at the age of two which was at Bereton Montesorri Nursery and Primary School. After passing her First School Leaving Certificate Examination, she proceeded for her secondary school education in the year 2001 at Federal Government Girls' College, Abuloma. Her tertiary education was at the prestigious University of Port Harcourt, where she bagged both a Bachelors and Masters degree in the year 2014 and 2019 respectively.

She has had various work experiences ranging from administrative, academic, research and roles in health non governmental organisations.

Her hobbies include surfing the internet, reading, listening to good music and trying out new things.
